Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about accounts, tasks, groups, and privacy.

How do I create an account?

Click Sign Up on the home page, provide a valid email and password, then confirm. We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage sign-ins.

I forgot my password. What now?

Password reset isn't available in-app yet. If you get locked out, please contact your instructor or project admin to assist with a reset.

What profile information is visible to others?

Only your display name and avatar appear in shared group views. Email addresses remain private and are never shown to other users.

How do I create a task?

Navigate to Add Task, fill in the name, description, and optional due date, then click Save. Your task appears instantly in your task list.

Can I edit or delete a task later?

Yes. Open a task's detail page and choose Edit to update fields or Delete to remove it permanently.

What is the difference between personal and group tasks?

Personal tasks are visible only to you. Group tasks are shared with all members of a group and can be collaboratively updated.

How do I create a group?

Go to Create Group, enter a name, course, and description. Share the generated Group ID with classmates so they can join it from the Groups page using Join Group.

How do I assign a task to a group member?

Assignments aren't supported yet. Group tasks are shared and visible to all members, and anyone in the group can update task details.

Will members be notified about new group tasks?

Members see updates when they next load the group dashboard. Real-time notifications may be added in future releases.

Do tasks send reminder notifications?

Currently reminders are not automatic. We recommend reviewing your dashboard daily. Scheduled notifications are on our roadmap.

How is my data stored?

All user and task data is stored securely in Firebase (Firestore + Authentication). Communication is encrypted over HTTPS.

Can I delete my account and data?

In-app account deletion isn't available yet. To request removal of your account and related data, please contact your instructor or project admin.

Do you share data with third parties?

No. Data is used only for providing core functionality (task and group management) and is never sold or shared externally.

Which browsers are supported?

Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Mobile modern browsers generally work; older versions may have limited support.

Why am I not seeing my recent changes?

Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, ensure your network connection is stable. Firebase sync may take a moment under poor connectivity.

How often is new functionality released?

We aim for iterative improvements. Significant features (like real-time alerts) will be announced on the dashboard.
